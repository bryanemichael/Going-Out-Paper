Variables on this sheet are: Service Provider, Type of Provider, Client side, Service Provider, City, Counterpart, Counterpart City, Type of Counterpart, Transaction Side, Chinese Acquirer  Name, Target Name, Target country, Target city, Acquiror city, Target primary US SIC code, Acquirer primary US SIC code, Deal Type, Deal status, Deal value in thousands of euro, Pre-deal target total assets (thousands of euro) and year.

Why don't we have annual Gephi data? My budget ran out before I could conduct any kind of serious network analysis of these data. Hopefully though, someone else will have the interest in showing how these networks of cities and advisors changed over time. Here are the data you need -- go to it!
